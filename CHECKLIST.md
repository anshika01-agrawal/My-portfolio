## üìù Content Writing Tips

### About Section
- Keep it conversational and genuine
- Highlight what makes you unique
- Show personality
- Include your interests beyond tech

### Project Descriptions
- Start with the problem you solved
- Explain your solution
- Mention technologies used
- Include impact/results if possible
- Keep it concise (2-3 sentences)

### Experience Descriptions
- Use action verbs (developed, led, implemented)
- Quantify achievements when possible
- Focus on impact and learning
- Keep professional but personable
